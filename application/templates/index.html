Making an Appointment

You don’t need a referral from your Psychiatrist to make an appointment to see one of our counselors at Well Heard.

We welcome any questions or requests for further information.

Initial Consultation

The therapy process begins with an initial consultation. The goal of this appointment is for you and your counselor to work together in getting a thorough understanding of your difficulties, personal and family history and strengths. It is the start of the process of building a trusting and collaborative relationship with your counselor.

Following this appointment, together you will develop an individualized formulation and treatment plan.

Therapy Process

Sessions are 50 minutes in duration and the frequency of appointments is individually tailored to meet your needs and goals. Appointments can be scheduled weekly, fortnightly and monthly.

Due to the pandemic, all meetings will be scheduled on Google Meet. Our counselors are bound by a professional code of ethics and are required to abide by principles of professional conduct, responsibilities and confidentiality.

Fees and Rebates

You may be eligible for Medicare or private health fund rebates depending on your referral and health fund provisions. Please feel free to contact us to discuss appointment fees and rebates.

There is a cancellation fee of 50% for appointments cancelled within 24 hours of the appointment time or for non-attended appointments. Unfortunately, no rebates are available for cancellation fees. Cancellations made before the 24 hour time frame will not incur a fee.
